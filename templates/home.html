Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your Privacy is Our Priority

AnonShare is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data or files. Our service operates entirely peer-to-peer.

What We Don't Collect

  • Personal information (names, emails, addresses)
  • File contents or metadata
  • IP addresses or connection logs
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies for tracking purposes

How It Works

All file transfers happen directly between your devices using WebRTC technology. Our server only serves the application code - it never sees or stores your files or connection data.

Technical Details

  • Connections are established peer-to-peer
  • All data is encrypted in transit
  • No central server processes your files
  • Connection codes are generated locally

Third Parties

We use STUN servers (Google's public STUN servers) only to help establish peer-to-peer connections. These servers do not store any data about your transfers.

Data Retention

Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to retain. All connection information is temporary and exists only during the active transfer session.

Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected, there are no data rights to exercise. You maintain complete control over your files and connections at all times.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us using our contact form.
